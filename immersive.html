How this page works

Built from the live catalog

Your browser reads the same catalog.json that powers the Immersive Video page, composes a poster for every title, and assembles a 3D galaxy of ~45 floating cards grouped into constellations by category — travel, sports, music, indie Theater films, and more.

A USDZ world, made on the fly

The galaxy is exported in your browser to USDZ and displayed with the HTML <model> element — rendered in true stereoscopic 3D on Apple Vision Pro. On a Mac, iPhone, or PC you get an interactive WebGL preview instead.

Step inside on visionOS 27

Safari's new Immersive API (requestImmersive()) carries the model beyond the browser window and places it around you at real-world scale — while this page stays visible as your remote control. Tap a category to spin that constellation in front of you.

Poster artwork is streamed from each film's original source and belongs to its rights holders. Titles link out from the main catalog page — this page is the view from inside.
