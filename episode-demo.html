Episode Layout Demo

Two approaches for showing series episodes — using Elevated as the example.

Option A

Horizontal Episode Strip

The series gets a standard card in the grid. Below it, a horizontally scrollable strip shows each episode with its own thumbnail. Familiar — mirrors how Apple TV itself presents series. Scrolls naturally for longer seasons.

Option C

Expandable Episodes

The series card shows an "Episodes" button. Clicking it expands a 2-column mini-grid of episode cards inline beneath the series card. The grid stays clean until you choose to explore.
