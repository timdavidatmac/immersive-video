🎬 Apple Immersive Video

News & Features

Breaking: Blackmagic URSA Cine Immersive 100G

Announced at NAB 2026, the URSA Cine Immersive 100G is the world's first immersive cinema camera designed for live production with Apple Immersive Video. Dual 8K×8K RGBW sensors, 16 stops of dynamic range, and 100G Ethernet output capable of delivering live immersive video in real time — paired with the new URSA Live Encoder for Apple ProRes output. Live sports, concerts, and events in Apple Immersive Video are now a reality.

Read the announcement →

Inside Apple Immersive Video — Ben Allan & Clara Chong

Justin Ryan (Spatial Insider) sits down with Ben Allan and Clara Chong — award-winning filmmakers and two of the leading experts in Apple Immersive Video. In this 34-minute conversation, they cover the full production journey: the Blackmagic URSA Cine Immersive workflow, DaVinci Resolve and Fairlight post-production, the creative and storytelling challenges unique to 180° space, and why this format has more in common with IMAX than traditional film or VR. Ben and Clara also discuss their book, Cinematic Immersive for Professionals.

A few numbers that put the format in perspective: Standard laser IMAX = 212 million pixels/sec. URSA Cine Immersive = 10.5 billion. ProRes masters run ~150 GB per minute. A 10-minute drama shoot produced 11.5 terabytes in two days.
