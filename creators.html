Get to Know Apple Immersive Video

Apple Immersive Video (AIV) is a new media format for adaptive, high-quality playback of stereoscopic large field-of-view experiences on visionOS, supported by dedicated tools and workflows for the highest fidelity, authenticity, and presence from capture to delivery. Combined with Apple Spatial Audio Format, Apple Immersive Video ensures accurate real-world scale and responsive projection with metadata that preserves the authenticity of the experience — unlocking a rich new visual medium for users on Apple Vision Pro from enterprise applications to entertainment.

Tools & Software

  • Apple Immersive Video Utility (AIVU) Apple's own tool for importing media, creating .aivu files, and preparing Apple Immersive Video content for playback or distribution. Free on the Mac App Store.
    User Guide
  • New Colorfront Immersive Utility Professional Mac app (Apple Silicon M1+, macOS 26 Tahoe+) for creating .aivu files from ProRes or MV-HEVC source. GPU-accelerated encoding, foveation, APAC spatial audio conversion, and multi-shot assembly via FCP XML. Free tier available. MV-HEVC License: $299/year. "From camera original to Apple Vision Pro — in one click."
    Mac App Store Press release Cinematography World
  • MainConcept MV-HEVC Encoder SDK Codec SDK 16.2 with DaVinci Resolve Studio and Colorfront integration for MV-HEVC encoding. April 2026.
  • New APMP Metadata Mac app for injecting, previewing, and exporting Apple Projected Media Profile (APMP) metadata — no command line required. Supports the full APMP specification including spatial, parametric, stereoscopic, and 360° workflows. Includes integrated VEXU (Video Extension Universal) metadata editing, video preview before export, and automatic metadata validation. Designed for developers and content creators working with immersive video pipelines and Vision Pro.
    Mac App Store Developer site
  • New Gracia — 4D Gaussian Splat Encoding & Distribution Processing and distribution pipeline for 4D Gaussian Splat (4DGS) volumetric content — Gracia handles processing only, not capture. Workflow: client shoots with a capture partner (Gracia refers clients to the nearest partner if needed), footage comes to Gracia, and delivery is typically 3–5 days as .mint/.PLY files. Gracia reconstructs temporally consistent 4DGS, removes unwanted objects, applies color LUTs, and compresses to near-lossless quality. One capture, three delivery targets: VFX (1M splats/frame), XR headsets (300–400K), and streaming (up to 125K). Output is compatible with Houdini, Cinema 4D, Nuke, Blender, After Effects, and DaVinci Resolve. View on Vision Pro with the Gracia: 4DGS Viewer app.
    gracia.ai Vision Pro App

Community-Developed Tools

The following tools were created by independent developers in the immersive video community. They're worth knowing about — but haven't been through commercial QA. Test before using in a production workflow.

  • AIME Generator Creates Apple Immersive Video lens profile files (.aime) for virtually any VR180 camera — not just the URSA Cine Immersive. Mac only.
  • New AIVU Trimmer macOS tool for trimming .aivu files without re-encoding — preserves all Apple immersive metadata (AIME, VenueDescriptor, spatial audio). Also exports: SBS MP4 for Meta Quest 3 preview (7680×3840, 60fps HEVC), rectilinear 16:9 for flat screen editing using a Blackmagic ST map EXR, and supports .cube LUT baking with live preview. Visual in/out point editor with timecode display at 45fps. Built by Elad Offer.
    GitHub LinkedIn post
  • New compress-usdz Small macOS command-line helper for reducing oversized .usdz files — auto-reduces textures and decimates the mesh using Blender and ImageMagick. Useful for getting large 3D models down to a reasonable size for AR Quick Look and Vision Pro. Built by Jeffreality.
    GitHub

🎧 Spatial Audio

Spatial audio is the other half of immersive video. ASAF (Apple Spatial Audio Format) and APAC (Apple Positional Audio Codec) are the audio counterparts to MV-HEVC — worth understanding alongside the image pipeline.

  • New Apple Spatial Audio Format — AAX Plugin Suite (Pro Tools) Official Apple production suite for Avid Pro Tools built on psychoacoustic principles. Delivers compelling, externalized audio experiences for Apple Immersive Video. Download from the AIV Resources page.
    Download from AIV Resources
  • Meet Apple Spatial Audio Format and APAC Official Apple session introducing ASAF and the Apple Positional Audio Codec — how spatial audio is captured, encoded, and delivered for Apple Vision Pro.
  • New fxguide — Believable and Immersive Audio By Mike Seymour, May 20, 2026. Two threads woven together: an FMX session on concert-crowd spatial audio recording by Emmy and Golden Reel-winning sound editor Jens Rosenlund Petersen (Bohemian Rhapsody, Saltburn) and a practical deep dive on ASAF for Apple Immersive Video. Includes a downloadable ASAF example file. "Believable cinematic worlds are not just built with pixels: they come alive through sound."
