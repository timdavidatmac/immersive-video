Create Immersive Content

Tools, cameras, spatial audio resources, and learning materials for Apple Immersive Video production — from capture through delivery.

Tools & Software

  • Apple Immersive Video Utility (AIVU) Apple's own tool for importing media, creating .aivu files, and preparing Apple Immersive Video content for playback or distribution. Free on the Mac App Store.
    User Guide
  • New Colorfront Immersive Utility Professional Mac app (Apple Silicon M1+, macOS 26 Tahoe+) for creating .aivu files from ProRes or MV-HEVC source. GPU-accelerated encoding, foveation, APAC spatial audio conversion, and multi-shot assembly via FCP XML. Free tier available. MV-HEVC License: $299/year. "From camera original to Apple Vision Pro — in one click."
    Mac App Store Press release Cinematography World
  • AIME Generator Creates Apple Immersive Video lens profile files (.aime) for virtually any VR180 camera — not just the URSA Cine Immersive. Mac only. Community-developed — evaluate before use.
  • MainConcept MV-HEVC Encoder SDK Codec SDK 16.2 with DaVinci Resolve Studio and Colorfront integration for MV-HEVC encoding. April 2026.
  • New APMP Metadata Mac app for injecting, previewing, and exporting Apple Projected Media Profile (APMP) metadata — no command line required. Supports the full APMP specification including spatial, parametric, stereoscopic, and 360° workflows. Includes integrated VEXU (Video Extension Universal) metadata editing, video preview before export, and automatic metadata validation. Designed for developers and content creators working with immersive video pipelines and Vision Pro.
    Mac App Store Developer site

Cameras

Pairs with the URSA Live Encoder for real-time ProRes output — live sports, concerts, and events in AIV are now possible. Announced NAB 2026.
    Camera Manual (PDF)

Underwater Housing

  • Gates Immersive Housing Developed in close collaboration with Apple and Blackmagic Design specifically for the URSA Cine Immersive. 12-inch AR-coated glass dome port. Precise camera alignment with custom system calibration — the calibration file is unique to each camera/housing/dome combination and applied in DaVinci Resolve Studio. Solo diver controls or optional surface tethers. Gates Seal Check and Buoyancy Trim systems. Available for sale and hire from Gates Underwater Products (Poway, CA) and Esprit Film and Television (Bristol, UK).
  • ACHTEL 3Deep Immersive Camera A different approach — dual submersible lenses in direct contact with water, eliminating the dome port and solving the stereoscopic fusion problems inherent to traditional underwater 3D. Designed for the ACHTEL 9×7 camera. Depth-rated to 60m. Precision-machined from titanium at approximately 20kg ready to dive. Available for sale and rental.
    Newsshooter deep dive

🎧 Spatial Audio

Spatial audio is the other half of immersive video. ASAF (Apple Spatial Audio Format) and APAC (Apple Positional Audio Codec) are the audio counterparts to MV-HEVC — worth understanding alongside the image pipeline.

  • Meet Apple Spatial Audio Format and APAC Official Apple session introducing ASAF and the Apple Positional Audio Codec — how spatial audio is captured, encoded, and delivered for Apple Vision Pro.
  • New fxguide — Believable and Immersive Audio By Mike Seymour, May 20, 2026. Two threads woven together: an FMX session on concert-crowd spatial audio recording by Emmy and Golden Reel-winning sound editor Jens Rosenlund Petersen (Bohemian Rhapsody, Saltburn) and a practical deep dive on ASAF for Apple Immersive Video. Includes a downloadable ASAF example file. "Believable cinematic worlds are not just built with pixels: they come alive through sound."
