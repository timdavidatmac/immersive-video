3D Models

Interactive USDZ models of immersive video hardware. Tap any model to view it in augmented reality on Apple Vision Pro, iPhone, or iPad — placed at real-world scale in your space.

How it works: Tap View in AR on a supported Apple device. On Apple Vision Pro, the model opens in Quick Look and is placed in your physical space at true scale. On iPhone or iPad, point your camera at a flat surface to place the model. No app required — powered by Apple AR Quick Look.
